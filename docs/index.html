Course Assessment

Final Project (100%) - Students choose one of three tracks based on their interests and career goals.
Technical Track
Build a working application using course technologies
  • DeFi protocol implementation
  • ML-based trading bot
  • Smart contract dApp
  • Payment system integration
Research Track
Academic research paper on emerging finance topics
  • Literature review (25+ sources)
  • Empirical analysis
  • Original contribution
  • Conference-style presentation
Business Track
Business case study and strategic analysis
  • Market analysis
  • Business model design
  • Financial projections
  • Regulatory compliance plan
