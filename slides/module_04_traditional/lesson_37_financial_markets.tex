\begin{frame}{Alternative Trading Systems (ATS)}
\begin{columns}[T]
\column{0.48\textwidth}
\textbf{Dark Pools}
\begin{itemize}
\item Private exchanges
\item No pre-trade transparency
\item Large block trades
\item Reduced market impact
\item 15-20\% US equity volume
\end{itemize}

\column{0.48\textwidth}
\textbf{Electronic Communication Networks}
\begin{itemize}
\item Automated matching
\item Direct market access
\item Lower fees
\item INET, Arca, BATS
\end{itemize}
\end{columns}

\vspace{1em}
\textbf{Fragmentation}: US equities trade across 16+ exchanges, 40+ dark pools
\bottomnote{Electronic trading has transformed market structure and efficiency.}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{Summary}
\begin{itemize}
\item Financial markets connect capital suppliers and users via intermediaries
\item Buy-side manages assets; sell-side facilitates transactions
\item Exchanges provide transparent price discovery; dark pools reduce impact
\item CCPs mitigate counterparty risk through novation and netting
\item CSDs enable efficient settlement via immobilization and book-entry
\item Regulators ensure market integrity, transparency, and investor protection
\item Technology infrastructure spans data feeds, trading systems, and connectivity
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
